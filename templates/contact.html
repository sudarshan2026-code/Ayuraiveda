💡 About This Project

AyurAI Veda is an educational AI-powered platform that integrates traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern artificial intelligence technology.

Key Features:

  • 🧠 Tridosha Intelligence Engine for health assessment
  • 💬 AyurVaani AI Chatbot for Ayurveda queries
  • 📊 AI-powered disease risk predictions
  • 📄 Personalized PDF health reports
  • 🔒 Privacy-focused (no data storage)

🎯 Purpose

This platform is designed for:

  • Students learning about Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS) and AI
  • Researchers in traditional medicine and technology integration
  • General users interested in preventive healthcare
  • Educational institutions implementing NEP 2020 guidelines
