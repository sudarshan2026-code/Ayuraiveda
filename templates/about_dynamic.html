🕉️ What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda, meaning "Science of Life," is a 5000-year-old holistic healing system originating from ancient India. It emphasizes prevention, balance, and harmony between body, mind, and spirit.

Unlike modern medicine that treats symptoms, Ayurveda identifies root causes of imbalance and provides personalized lifestyle guidance.

⚖️ The Tridosha Theory

According to Ayurveda, every individual is governed by three fundamental energies called Doshas:

🌬️ Vata (Air + Space)

Governs: Movement, creativity, nervous system, circulation

Qualities: Dry, light, cold, mobile, irregular

Imbalance Signs: Anxiety, insomnia, dry skin, constipation, restlessness

Balance Tips: Warm foods, routine, oil massage, grounding practices

🔥 Pitta (Fire + Water)

Governs: Metabolism, digestion, transformation, intelligence

Qualities: Hot, sharp, intense, oily, penetrating

Imbalance Signs: Acidity, inflammation, anger, skin rashes, excessive heat

Balance Tips: Cooling foods, avoid spicy items, meditation, moderate exercise

🌊 Kapha (Water + Earth)

Governs: Structure, stability, lubrication, immunity

Qualities: Heavy, slow, cool, oily, stable

Imbalance Signs: Weight gain, lethargy, congestion, depression, excess mucus

Balance Tips: Light foods, regular exercise, avoid dairy, wake early

🧬 Scientific Basis

Modern research validates many Ayurvedic principles:

  • Turmeric (Curcumin) - Anti-inflammatory properties validated by 10,000+ studies
  • Ashwagandha - Proven adaptogen for stress reduction (cortisol regulation)
  • Pranayama - Improves HRV (Heart Rate Variability) and autonomic balance
  • Circadian rhythm alignment - Matches Ayurvedic daily routine (Dinacharya)
  • Gut-brain axis - Validates Ayurveda's emphasis on digestive health
