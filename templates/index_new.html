Understanding Tridosha

Three fundamental energies that govern all physical and mental processes

🌬️

Vata

Air + Space

Governs movement, creativity, and nervous system. Imbalance causes anxiety, dry skin, and irregular digestion.

🔥

Pitta

Fire + Water

Governs metabolism, digestion, and transformation. Imbalance causes acidity, inflammation, and irritability.

🌊

Kapha

Water + Earth

Governs structure, stability, and immunity. Imbalance causes weight gain, lethargy, and congestion.
